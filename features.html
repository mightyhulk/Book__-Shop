Website Features


1 For the first time, Buyer registers with the website to generate a valid username and password

2 Login web page Once the registration is successful, the buyer can login and go for searching and shopping various products available.

3 After login, Buyer can view all the recommended products on the homepage compiled by system based on users past shopping information and other factors mentioned by the user

4 Search Option -Buyer can search for some products and select the desired product and view its details and add to cart if he/she wishes to buy the product.

5 Buy Option Once buyer will click on buy button he will be redirected to Different shopping website on which the product is available at cheapest price

6 Order Placing After redirecting to that page buyer can purchase the product and pay the required amount then product will be delivered to his desired address.

7 Payment Method Payment should be accepted on redirected website in all format google pay, amazon pay, freecharge, net banking, debit card, credit card, pay on delivery
